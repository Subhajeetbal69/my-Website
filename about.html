About iEducation

Transforming rural education through technology, innovation, and equal access to learning opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower children with the knowledge, skills and confidence they need to build a better future for themselves and their communities.

Who We Are

iEducation is a social initiative dedicated to transforming rural education through technology and inclusive learning opportunities. We believe every child deserves quality education, regardless of where they live or their background.

Many rural children face challenges such as lack of digital access, shortage of trained teachers, and limited awareness of career paths. We work to bridge this gap and help students become confident, curious, and future-ready learners.

What We Do

We design programs that build strong academic foundations while also focusing on digital literacy, creativity, and life skills.

  • Digital learning and smart-class support
  • Coding, STEM and innovation workshops
  • Career guidance and scholarship awareness
  • Teacher training and resource support
  • Community engagement for rural education

Our Core Values

  • Equity: every child matters
  • Innovation: creative solutions for real problems
  • Integrity: honesty and transparency
  • Community First: we work with people, not just for them
  • Sustainability: long-term impact

Our Vision

A world where every child — rural or urban — has equal access to technology, education and opportunities to succeed.
